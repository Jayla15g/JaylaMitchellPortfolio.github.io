About Me

My name is Jayla Mitchell and I am a first-generation college student who will be graduating from the Florida State University with a Bachelor of Science in Information, Communication and Technology in May of 2019. I also will be receiving a certificate in Health Information Technology. Listening to my mother, I was a pre-med student my first two years of college and recently changed my major to ICT in Fall of 2017 after taking a few Information Technology courses that intrigued my interest into this field. In this short amount of time that I have been in this field, I have interned as a Web Developer, System Administrator, have done little database work for a company, and I am currently interning with FSU’s Wireless Network team. I am a dedicated student with a great personality who enjoys learning. I am very outgoing, always smiling and will work well with anyone. I went into my internships with little to no knowledge in the position, but I was certain to work hard to be one of their best students in any position they put me in. Through this, I’ve became the person to say “yes” and then figure it out late. Either way I will get it done. I am always looking for a way to exercise my skills and to make them better. Though I have many technical skills, I have recently found a new interest in leading teams and want to work towards becoming a project manager. In my IT leadership course at FSU, I learned the many skills of being a great and successful leader and how much I enjoy working in teams. I have also found interest in User Experience Design. Whether I land a job as a project manager or find myself in a position where I am using my technical skills, I am excited to utilize my technical and communication skills to contribute to a company while also gaining a great learning experience! I hope to find a company who gives me the necessary tools that will push me towards professional and personal growth as I will do my best to bring their company growth. Outside of work, I enjoy doing community service, walking through parks, traveling, reading, and writing short stories. One day I hope to learn and master adobe animate so that I am able to turn my short stories into short films.
